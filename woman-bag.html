Women's Bags

Key Principles for Choosing a Bag:


  • Size and Dimensions:

    Before choosing a bag, consider its purpose. For example, large bags are suitable for university, work, or mothers with young children, while smaller bags are ideal for parties and formal events. Therefore, compare the bag's size with the items you anticipate needing to carry before making your choice.

  • Material:

    Bags come in various materials such as leather, canvas, mesh, or jute. Typically, jute bags are used in summer, while leather bags are versatile and suitable for all seasons. Leather bags are also more durable, while canvas bags are lightweight and convenient to carry.

  • Color:

    Pay attention to your outfit when selecting a bag color, as it plays a significant role in your overall style. For instance, if your outfit is monochromatic, you can opt for a dark or contrasting bag to add vibrancy to your look.

  • Type of Bag:

    There are various types of bags, such as crossbody, shoulder bags, backpacks, and more. The type of bag depends on individual needs and usage. For example, backpacks are typically used for work, school, or university purposes.
